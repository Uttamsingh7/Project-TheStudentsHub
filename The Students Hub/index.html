Vision

The vision of thestudenthub is that we are going to provide the facilities for the students to work on their weakness regarding to the study and provide them a better environment through thestudenthub to clear their doubts and enhance their performance to work better or to improve their studies..it is a site where we are going to provide many options to the students likewise a option of webinar and a notes section with a extra section where the alumni or the seniors can interact with the juniors and the freshers where they can share their experience to them how they got success and the placement their several projects and provide them a roadmap for their courses and to get success in the life...webinar section is mainly for the guests lectures and some of the known persons who can inspire students give them a better idea about the ongoing situations by which adopting them they can get a better tomorrow... For every department of students we have added a test or quiz contest in this where anyone can test their skill in the specific field of their own.after that based on the results they get can improve themselves better..
